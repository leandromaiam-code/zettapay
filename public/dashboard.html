Sign in with an offline signature

Prove wallet ownership without connecting anything. Paste your address, sign the challenge message in whatever wallet you already use (Phantom, Solflare, hardware, mobile), and paste the signature back. ZettaPay never asks for permissions and never custodies funds.

2 · Sign this message in your wallet

In Phantom: Settings → Developer mode → Sign message → paste and sign. In Solflare: tap your wallet name → Settings → Sign message. Hardware wallets: use the companion app's offline signing tool.

How it works
  1. Paste the public address of the wallet you registered.
  2. Sign the challenge message offline — in your wallet, on your device.
  3. We verify the Ed25519 signature locally and unlock the dashboard.

We never request wallet permissions or open a connect prompt. Your keys never leave your wallet.
