FAQ

Quick answers

The three questions every merchant asks before signing up.

How do I get started?
Three steps, ~5 minutes:
  1. Click Start Free and connect your Phantom wallet — no email or card required.
  2. Copy your API key and the <script> tag from your dashboard, or install @zettapay/sdk.
  3. Drop the embed onto your checkout page. Your first USDC payment lands in your wallet in under 2 seconds.

Full walkthrough lives in /docs/quickstart and the embed install in /docs/embed.

What about KYC?
ZettaPay is non-custodial. Funds move directly from payer to merchant on Solana — we never hold the USDC, so we are not a money-services business in the regulatory sense. You can register a merchant with just a wallet.

KYC only enters the picture when a buyer needs to convert fiat → USDC via the MoonPay onramp. In that flow, MoonPay performs KYC on the buyer (not on the merchant) according to the buyer's jurisdiction. Crypto-native buyers paying directly with their wallet skip KYC entirely.

Mainnet launches and ongoing compliance posture are tracked in /launch and the public status endpoint.

Mainnet support?
Yes — ZettaPay is live on Solana mainnet with a curated cohort of 10 beta merchants, each capped at $10,000 in volume for the first 30-day window. New signups go through an allowlist while we widen the gates.

Devnet remains open for everyone to test and integrate against. Once Cohort 2 opens, caps are lifted in stages. Track live cohort utilization at /launch or via /beta/status.

In V1 the chain is Solana and the currency is USDC. Multi-chain and additional stablecoins are on the roadmap after PMF.
