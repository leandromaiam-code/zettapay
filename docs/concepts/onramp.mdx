---
title: "Fiat onramp"
description: "Card → USDC funding flow via MoonPay for first-time crypto buyers."
---

Most consumers do not have USDC on hand. ZettaPay integrates with
[MoonPay](https://moonpay.com) so a payer can fund their wallet with a
debit card or bank transfer, then complete the payment in a single
checkout flow.

## Why MoonPay

- **KYC delegation.** ZettaPay is not a Money Services Business. MoonPay
  handles all KYC/AML obligations for fiat → crypto conversion.
- **Global coverage.** 160+ countries, dozens of fiat currencies.
- **Webhook parity.** MoonPay confirms in a webhook event we relay to
  the merchant on the same delivery infrastructure as native ZettaPay
  events.

## Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Merchant generates an onramp URL">
    Call MCP tool `create_onramp_url` (or hit the equivalent HTTP
    endpoint) with the target merchant id, fiat currency and amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payer completes KYC + checkout">
    The payer is redirected to MoonPay's hosted flow. They verify
    identity and pay with card or bank transfer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="MoonPay settles USDC into the payer's wallet">
    Settlement typically completes in two to ten minutes depending on
    payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="ZettaPay receives `payment.confirmed` from MoonPay">
    The signature is validated against `MOONPAY_WEBHOOK_SECRET` (5-min
    tolerance window). The event is recorded and a corresponding
    `onramp.completed` webhook fires to the merchant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Merchant flow continues">
    The merchant can now invoke `POST /pay` to debit the freshly funded
    wallet, or wait for the payer to retry the original action.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configuration

The MoonPay integration is driven by environment variables on the
ZettaPay backend:

| Variable | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `MOONPAY_API_KEY` | MoonPay API key. |
| `MOONPAY_WEBHOOK_SECRET` | HMAC secret for incoming webhook validation. |
| `MOONPAY_ENV` | `sandbox` or `production`. |

Merchants do not configure MoonPay directly. The protocol owns the
MoonPay relationship and forwards confirmation events.
